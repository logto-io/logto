add captcha bot protection

You can now enable CAPTCHA bot protection for your sign-in-experience flow with providers like Google ReCAPTCHA enterprise and Cloudflare Turnstile.

To enable CAPTCHA bot protection, you need to:

1. Go to Console > Security > Bot protection.
2. Select the CAPTCHA provider you want to use.
3. Configure the CAPTCHA provider.
4. Save the settings.

Then take a preview of your sign-in-experience flow to see the CAPTCHA in action.

This feature is available for paid plans only, but you can try it out with a dev tenant.
